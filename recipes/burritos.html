Veggie Burrito

Veggie Burrito: just under 300 calories

This is the one of the best vegetarian recipes. When you crave a crispy fried veggie burrito. With plenty of toppings for every appetite. This meal is guaranteed to keep you full for a long time.

Ingredients

  • 1 Wrap
  • 1/2 Black Beans
  • 1 eggs
  • 1/4 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1 Tbsp. hummus
  • 1/4 cup green onions
  • 1/4 cup Rice

Steps need to make the Veggie Burrito

  1. Put frying pan on low setting.
  2. Take the wrap and put all the toppings inside.
  3. Grill the wrap both sides and your done.
