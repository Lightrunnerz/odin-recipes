Delicious Vegetarian Tofu and Eggs

Tofu and Eggs: just under 300 calories

This is the one of the best vegetarian recipes. When you crave a crispy tender bite of tofu and eggs. With just over 15% protein. This meal is guaranteed to keep you full for a long time.

Ingredients

  • 500 grams tofu
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup diced celery
  • 1 Tbsp. soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 4 tbsp Olive Oil

Steps need to make the Vegetarian Tofu and Eggs

  1. Put frying pan on low setting.
  2. Use two plates with a book on top to squish the tofu for 15 mins.
  3. Beat the eggs into a pulp and put them in the frying pan.
  4. When the tofu has been squished for 15 mins. Take the tofu and cut it up into small pieces. And put in the frying pan.
  5. Stir for 10 mins and you are done.
