Veggie Pizza

Veggie Pizza: just under $8 dollars

This is the one of the best vegetarian pizza's. When you crave a crispy supermarket pizza. With plenty of toppings for every appetite. This pizza is guaranteed to keep you full for a long time.

Ingredients

  • 1 store bought cheese pizza
  • 1/2 Cup Olives
  • 1/2 Cup Mushrooms
  • 1/2 cup Onions

Steps needed to make the Veggie Pizza

  1. Bake at 400 degrees for 15 mins.
  2. You cheated a little not making it from scratch but you feel better because you added Olives, Mushrooms, and Onions.
